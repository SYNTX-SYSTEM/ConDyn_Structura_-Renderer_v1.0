4. CONSTRAINT HIERARCHY

All structural transitions are governed by three transformation rules.

These rules ensure invariant preservation, temporal integrity, and non-destructive state evolution.

Transformation Rules

Rule Definition Guarantee
Monotonic Accumulation State transitions append structural information without erasure. Complete historical traceability.
Axiom Priority Immutable invariants override conflicting updates. No silent override of institutional commitments.
Temporal Precedence Constraints propagate forward across all subsequent transitions. Later inputs cannot invalidate earlier commitments without formal amendment.

Axiom Registry

The Immutable Axiom Registry defines structural invariants.
